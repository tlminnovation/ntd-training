SKINAPP TRAINING COURSE - QUICK REFERENCE GUIDE
==============================================

COURSE AT A GLANCE
------------------
Total Duration: 45-60 minutes
Modules: 5 + Final Assessment
Pass Rate: 80%
Format: Interactive H5P content
Target: Medical staff collecting clinical images

MODULE BREAKDOWN
----------------

MODULE 1: PROJECT OVERVIEW (10 min)
Learning Objectives:
- Understand the AI training project goals
- Know your role and responsibilities
- Recognize the governance structure

Key Points:
- Project goal: Train AI for leprosy/skin NTD diagnosis
- 4-level governance: WHO → TLM → Partners → Clinicians
- Your responsibility: Patient welfare comes first

MODULE 2: CLINICAL PHOTOGRAPHY (12 min)
Learning Objectives:
- Capture high-quality diagnostic images
- Complete required metadata fields
- Handle sensitive body areas appropriately

Key Points:
- Use macro mode, good lighting, proper focus
- 10 required metadata fields (including Fitzpatrick scale)
- Special care for private areas: specific consent, draping, privacy

MODULE 3: INFORMED CONSENT (15 min)
Learning Objectives:
- Navigate real-world consent challenges
- Recognize when consent is not voluntary
- Respond appropriately to consent withdrawal

Key Points:
- Consent is ongoing, not one-time
- Use professional interpreters
- Withdrawal must be respected immediately
- Watch for red flags: confusion, fear, looking to others

MODULE 4: PRIVACY & ANONYMIZATION (10 min)
Learning Objectives:
- Remove identifying information from images
- Understand what must be hidden
- Follow data protection protocols

Key Points:
- Remove: faces, names, tattoos, jewelry, backgrounds
- Keep: clinical lesions, context, scale indicators
- Check twice before submitting
- Use secure upload, delete from device after

MODULE 5: SAFEGUARDING (10 min)
Learning Objectives:
- Protect dignity during photography
- Identify vulnerable populations
- Respond to safeguarding concerns
- Know when to report

Key Points:
- 5 principles: Respect, Continuous Consent, Vulnerable Protection, Dignity, Mandatory Reporting
- Children need guardian consent + assent
- Stop if any safeguarding concerns arise
- Report suspected abuse per institutional protocols

FINAL ASSESSMENT (5-10 min)
- 10 questions
- 80% pass rate
- Covers all modules
- Retakes allowed

TROUBLESHOOTING
---------------
Q: Content not loading?
A: Check browser compatibility, clear cache

Q: Can't upload H5P files?
A: Ensure platform has H5P plugin installed

Q: Need to translate content?
A: H5P supports multi-language; export, translate, re-import

Q: Want to add videos?
A: H5P supports video embedding; ensure patient consent for any video content

Q: Tracking not working?
A: Enable completion tracking in LMS settings

KEY TERMS QUICK REFERENCE
-------------------------
Fitzpatrick Scale: I-VI scale for skin tone
Ridley-Jopling: Leprosy classification system
Assent: Agreement from child (vs legal consent from guardian)
De-identification: Removing identifying information
Paucibacillary/Multibacillary: Leprosy operational classifications
Safeguarding: Protecting from harm, abuse, neglect
