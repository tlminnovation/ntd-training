SkinApp Training Course

WHO SkinNTD AI Image Collection Initiative

Course Overview: This interactive training prepares medical staff to collect clinical images for AI training while maintaining patient dignity, privacy, and safeguarding standards.

Course Modules

Module 1: Project Overview & Objectives

~10 minutes | Course Presentation

  • Project goals and AI training objectives
  • Governance structure (WHO → TLM → Partners → Clinicians)
  • Your role and responsibilities
  • Knowledge check quiz

Module 2: Clinical Photography Best Practices

~12 minutes | Course Presentation

  • Image quality requirements
  • 10 required metadata fields
  • Photography techniques (do's and don'ts)
  • Special considerations for private body areas
  • Knowledge check quiz

Module 3: Informed Consent Procedures

~15 minutes | Branching Scenario

  • Scenario 1: Language barriers and interpreters
  • Scenario 2: Patient hesitation and discomfort
  • Scenario 3: Withdrawal of consent
  • Interactive decision-making

Module 4: Anonymization & Privacy Protection

~10 minutes | Course Presentation

  • Why anonymization matters
  • What must be removed (faces, tattoos, jewelry, backgrounds)
  • Before/after examples
  • Technical safeguards
  • Knowledge check quiz

Module 5: Safeguarding Principles

~10 minutes | Course Presentation

  • 5 principles of dignity
  • Vulnerable populations (children, elderly, disabled)
  • Red flags indicating coercion
  • Mandatory reporting requirements
  • Knowledge check quiz

Final Assessment

~10 minutes | Question Set

  • 10 comprehensive questions
  • Covers all 5 modules
  • 80% pass rate required
  • Multiple choice and True/False questions
  • Unlimited retakes allowed

Total Course Duration: 45-60 minutes

How to Deploy

  1. Choose your platform: Moodle, H5P.com, WordPress, or Drupal
  2. Import the H5P JSON files
  3. Configure completion tracking
  4. Customize with your branding (optional)
  5. Test with pilot group
  6. Launch to medical staff

Integration with KoboToolbox/REDCap

Recommended workflow:

  1. Staff completes H5P training course
  2. Passes final assessment (80%)
  3. Receives completion certificate/code
  4. Enters code in KoboToolbox/REDCap form
  5. Gains access to image collection system

Pre-Deployment Checklist

  • □ Review content with clinical team
  • □ Test on chosen platform
  • □ Customize if needed (local examples, branding)
  • □ Translate to local languages if needed
  • □ Test mobile responsiveness
  • □ Verify completion tracking
  • □ Pilot with small group
  • □ Gather feedback
  • □ Launch to all staff

Key Features

  • Interactive: Branching scenarios and decision-making
  • Comprehensive: Covers all technical specification requirements
  • Practical: Real-world clinical scenarios
  • Free: H5P is open source, no licensing costs
  • Mobile-friendly: Works on tablets and phones
  • Trackable: Completion and scoring data
  • Customizable: Can be modified and translated
Important Reminder: This training emphasizes that patient welfare always comes first. When in doubt about any ethical concern, STOP and consult with supervisors.
