SKINAPP TRAINING COURSE - CONTENT SUMMARY
==========================================

COURSE OVERVIEW
---------------
Complete interactive training course for medical staff participating in the WHO SkinNTD AI Image Collection Initiative.

Total Duration: 45-60 minutes
Modules: 5 learning modules + 1 final assessment
Format: Text-based content ready for H5P conversion
Target: Medical staff, clinicians, healthcare workers

   - 4 detailed scenarios
